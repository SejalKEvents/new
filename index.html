Meet Sejal

Over a decade of luxury events experience

Hi, I'm Sejal — founder of Sejal K Events and a passionate advocate for celebrations that feel truly, deeply you. With over 15 years in the events industry, I've had the privilege of working at some of London's most iconic luxury venues, including the JW Marriott Grosvenor House, the Jumeirah Carlton Tower, and the London Marriott Hotel County Hall.

That foundation in world-class hospitality taught me the standards and behind-the-scenes precision that make a great event feel effortless. In 2024, I channelled everything I'd learned into founding Sejal K Events — a bespoke design and consultancy agency built around the belief that your day should reflect exactly who you are.
